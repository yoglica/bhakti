Introduction

Satya Sai Baba, who has been a source of inspiration for people of all religions, is born on November 23, 1926. His views were very influential, he believed that it is the duty of every individual to ensure that all people have access to the basic necessities of life. On his death anniversary (April 24), we dive deep into spirituality by following his teachings to lead a purposeful life.

Here are presented to the readers 20 priceless thoughts of Sathya Sai Baba, which will change the way you live.

20 Sathya Sai Baba Quotes

01
"If you always keep full faith in GOD, you will definitely get his grace. Compassion removes the pain of karma. The Lord has the power to entirely free a man from Karma."
— Sathya Sai Baba
02
"Money comes and goes. Morality comes and grows."
— Sathya Sai Baba
03
"Always help. Never give sorrow."
— Sathya Sai Baba
04
"Learn to give, not take. Learn to serve, not the state."
— Sathya Sai Baba
05
"Duty is God and work is worship. Karma like a straw is also a flower placed at the feet of God."
— Sathya Sai Baba
06
"Love everyone. Serve everyone."
— Sathya Sai Baba
07
"Always remember these three things - Don't trust the world. Do not forget the Lord. Don't be afraid of death."
— Sathya Sai Baba
08
"You are not one but three beings – one who you think you are, the other who you think you are, and the third who you really are."
— Sathya Sai Baba
09
"All actions arise from thoughts. So thoughts are those that have value."
— Sathya Sai Baba
10
"Living with the Lord is education, living for the Lord is service. Living with the Lord is the realization."
— Sathya Sai Baba
11
"Ego lives in taking and forgetting. Love lives in giving and forgiving."
— Sathya Sai Baba
12
"Duty without love is reprehensible. Duty with love is desirable. Dutyless love is divine."
— Sathya Sai Baba
13
"Education makes the heart soft. If the heart is hard, the right to be educated cannot be claimed."
— Sathya Sai Baba
14
"Love is truth in the form of thought. Love as an emotion is non-violence. Love in the form of action is right conduct. Love as understanding is peace."
— Sathya Sai Baba
15
"The purpose of education cannot be earning money. The development of good values can be the only goal of education."
— Sathya Sai Baba
16
"Start ahead of time. Slow down reach safely."
— Sathya Sai Baba
17
"Haste gives futility. Uselessness gives anxiety. That is why you should not be in a rush."
— Sathya Sai Baba
18
"Start the day with love. Spend the day with love. Fill the day with love only. End the day with love. This is the way towards the Lord."
— Sathya Sai Baba
19
"If there is a loss of money then there is no loss. If there is a loss of health then some loss has happened. If there is loss of character, then understand that everything has become weak."
— Sathya Sai Baba
20
"You take one step towards me, I will take hundred steps towards you."
— Sathya Sai Baba

Conclusion

Sathya Sai Baba's teachings are a guiding light for humanity. His words on love, service, duty, and spirituality transcend religion and culture, offering timeless wisdom for living a meaningful and purposeful life.

These 20 quotes remind us that true happiness lies not in material possessions but in love, compassion, and selfless service. By following Sai Baba's teachings, we can transform ourselves and contribute to making the world a better place for all.
