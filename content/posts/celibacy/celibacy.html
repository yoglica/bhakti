Introduction to Brahmacharya

Brahmacharya can be split into Brahman and Charya.

  • Brahman: Supreme creator according to Hindu scripture
  • Charya: Practice

One who follows procedures which leads to Brahman is called a Brahmachari.

The Four Stages of Life

According to ancient texts of Hindus, there are 4 stages of life:

  1. Brahmacharya (Student Life)
  2. Grahastha (Householder Life)
  3. Vanaprasta (Retired Life)
  4. Sanyasa (Renounced Life)

Everyone was required to practice this from the age of 14 years to 20 years. A person can be a life-long brahmachari. There are lots of rules and regulations to follow this.
