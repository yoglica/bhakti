What is Brahma Muhurta?

  1. Brahma or Brahman is the Creator of universe.
  2. Muhurta means the time period.

The 'time of Brahma' is called Brahma Muhurta. Brahmamuhurta is a time that starts one hour and 36 minutes (1 hour:36 min) before sunrise and lasts 48 minutes. There is a common doubt that Brahmamuhurta time is every country. The answer is No. It changes depending on the sunrise.

Ashtanga Hridayam
brahma muhurtam uttishthet
swastho rakshartham Ayusha:tatra
sarvartha shantyartham
smareccha madhusudanam

Meaning of the above sloka: To maintain excellent health and to live a long life, one should wake up at the Brahma Muhurta.

What to Do in Brahma Muhurta?

Do only 4 important things to do in Brahma Muhurta time:

  1. Sandhya Vandan
  2. Meditation
  3. Prayer
  4. Study

Sandhya Vandan done in Vedic way is most appropriate. After that meditation then prayer. The student should study after the worship. This time is considered best for study. If you practice it for a month you will definitely see the change within yourselves.

At the same time, the amount of oxygen (pranavayu) in the atmosphere is the highest (41 percent), which is important for the purification of the lungs. By getting pure air, the mind and brain also remain healthy. At such a time cleaning of the city is prohibited.

According to Ayurveda, the air flowing at this time has been called nectar like. By getting up and walking in the Brahma Muhurta, there is communication of Sanjivani Shakti in the body.

This time has also been said to be the best for study, because when we wake up in the morning after resting at night, the body and mind remain energetic and fresh. If the level of oxygen is also high in the morning, then it provides extra energy to the brain, due to which the studied things easily go into the memory fund.
