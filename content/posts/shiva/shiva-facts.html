Who is Lord Shiva?

Lord Shiva is worshiped as the most powerful deity in Hinduism. It is believed that the existence of Lord Shiva existed even before the beginning of this universe. According to the belief of Hindu religion, this creation is in the hands of Trinity from birth to death.

Lord Brahma, Lord Vishnu, Lord Shiva come in these trinity whom we know as Trimurti. Of these, Lord Brahma creates this universe, Lord Vishnu operates this universe and Lord Shiva is the destroyer of creation and evils, darkness etc. Of these three main deities, Lord Shiva is considered the most powerful.
